Description

The idea of Center For Learning and Student Success(CLASS) was birthed in early 2019. We noticed how difficult it was to connect teachers who can teach various courses and students who want to learn the courses. We accept Nigeria's most promising corps members from various states. The Center for Learning and Student Success is a transformational leadership skills beyond the classrooms in the communities we serve. It is a free training for secondary and primary school students. Our corp members are friendly, screened and vetted for high quality. We are trusted by happy parents and studeents with more than 80% success rate. We help improvr performance in class, get help with homework, build confidence in weak areas and prepare our students for the nearest future
